Recipe #1

4 Cheese Grilled Cheese

  • Time: 5-10 minutes total.
  • Serves: A regular sized grilled cheese with 4 different cheesy flavors; inside and ontop of a fluffy, toasty, and golden brown bread.
Ingredients Quantity
Melted Butter 1 tbsp (15ml)
Chopped Parsley Leaves 1 tbsp (15ml)
Grated Parmesean Cheese 1 tbsp (15)ml
Minced Garlic 1 clove
Black pepper pinch
Fresh Crusty Bread 4 slices (1 cm thick)
Cheddar Cheese 4 slices (medium sized)
Mozzarella cheese 1/4 cup (50 ml)
Gruyer Cheese 1/4 cup (50 ml)

Step 1: Blend butter with parmesan, garlic, and pepper until smooth.

Step 2: Spread the butter on one side of the bread, while putting cheddar on the other sides of the two bread.

Step 3: Toss and spread the mozzarella and Gruyer cheese on cheddar bread.

Step 4: Cook on a stove or in an oven for about 5 minutes or until the bread is a golded brown.

Step 5: Wait to cool for 2 minutes and enjoy. any type of cheese can be put for this sandwitch.

Recipe #2

Spagettie and Meetsauce

  • Time: 25-50 minutes total.
  • Serves: Spagetti with italian noodles with a rich marinarra sauce with bits of meat.
Ingredients Quantity
Dry spagetti (Uncooked) 12 ounces
Canola Oil 2 tbsp0
Chopped Onion 1 cup
Minced Garlic 2 tbsp
Ground Round Beef (85% lean) 1 pound
(Hunt's)Traditional Pasta sauce 1 Can (24 oz each)
Grated Parmesan Cheese Optional

Step 1: Boil water in pan until bubbling. Than put all the pasta into the boiling wanter until their loose for flexible (Follow box intructions).

Step 2: Meanwhile, add the oil into a skillet over mediam-high heat. Add onions and garlic. Cooking them for about 5 minutes while stirring frequently, or until the onions are tender.

Step 3: Add beef: cook for 7 minutes or until the meat is crumbled and no longer pink, stirring occasionally. Drain the oil and juices afterward.

Step 4: Add pasta sauce to skillet; stir together. Simmer and cook over a medium-low heat for 10 minutes or until hot.

Step 5: Finally, take the spaggetti from water and dry out, putting it on plate and pour the meatsauce on the spaggetti. Sprinkle cheese on it if desired.

Recipe #3

Scrabled Eggs

  • Time: 5 minutes total cook time.
  • Serves: Eggs in a scrambled fashion, with the yoke mixed to a fluffy texter.
Ingredients Quantity
Eggs 4
Milk 1/4 cups
Salt and Pepper A pinch if desired
Butter 2 tsp

Step 1: Beat eggs, milk, and pepper in medium bowl until blended.

Step 2: Heat butter in large nonstick skillet over medium heat until hot.

Step 3:Pour in egg mixture. As eggs begin to set, gently pull the eggs across the pan with a spatula, forming large soft curds.

Step 4: Continue cooking-pulling, lifting and folding eggs-until thickend and no visible liquid eggs remains.

Step 5: Do not stir constantly, Remove from heat, add pepper if preffered. Serve immediatly when hot.
